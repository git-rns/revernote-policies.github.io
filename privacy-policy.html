1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Revernote ("we", "our", "us") collects, uses, and protects your information through the use of our mobile application ("the App"). By accessing or using the App, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

We are committed to protecting your privacy and complying with applicable data protection laws, including the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), and other global standards.

2. Definitions

  • Personal Data refers to any information that identifies you as an individual, including your name, email address, or phone number.
  • Processing means any action performed on Personal Data (e.g., collection, storage, use).
  • Device Data includes information like your device model, operating system, crash logs, and app version.

3. Information We Collect

3.1 Account Information

When you sign up for Revernote using email/password via Firebase Authentication, we collect and store the following:

  • First Name & Last Name
  • Email Address
  • Phone Number (if provided)

This data is stored securely in Firebase Cloud Firestore.

3.2 Voice Recordings & Transcriptions

When you use the journaling feature, your voice is recorded and transcribed locally on your device.

Revernote does not upload voice recordings or transcripts to any remote server.

You maintain full control over these files.

3.3 Analytics & Usage Data

We use Firebase Analytics to collect anonymised or pseudonymised data such as:

  • Frequency of app usage
  • Screens visited
  • Session duration
  • Crash data

This information helps us improve app performance and user experience.

4. Permissions Used

Revernote requires the following Android permissions:

PermissionPurpose
RECORD_AUDIOEnables voice journaling functionality. Recordings remain on your device.
FOREGROUND_SERVICEAllows background audio processing for a seamless experience.
RECEIVE_BOOT_COMPLETEDRestores reminders or scheduled tasks after reboot.
VIBRATEEnables haptic feedback for journaling events or notifications.
SCHEDULE_EXACT_ALARMLets Revernote set exact reminder or summary alarms.
USE_FULL_SCREEN_INTENTAllows full-screen alerts for important journaling reminders.

We only use these permissions for their clearly stated purposes and do not access other device data.

5. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used for the following purposes:

  • To authenticate your account
  • To personalise and operate core app features
  • To provide technical support
  • To send relevant notifications (e.g., daily journal reminders)
  • To monitor app performance, stability, and improve functionality

6. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR Compliance)

We process your personal data based on:

  • Contractual necessity – to provide core app functionality
  • Legitimate interests – to improve the app experience
  • Consent – when explicitly required for optional features

7. Data Sharing & Third Parties

We do not sell your personal data.
We only share your data with Firebase, which provides:

  • Authentication (Firebase Auth)
  • Database storage (Cloud Firestore)
  • Crash and usage analytics (Firebase Analytics)

Firebase is GDPR and CCPA compliant. Their privacy policy is available at:
https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy

Revernote is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe we have collected such data, contact us to request deletion.

11. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organisational safeguards to protect your data from unauthorised access, misuse, or alteration, including:

However, no digital system is 100% secure. We recommend keeping your device secure and not sharing your login credentials.
